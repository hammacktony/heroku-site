My Projects

A few of my personal projects

Comic News

One of my projects is the "Batman Universe Comic News Aggregator." This uses BeautifulSoup4 to scour eight of the top comic news sites for Batman-related articles. It displays the first three pages of results of each site.

The eight news sites currently supported: bleedingcool.com, cbr.com, comicbook.com, comicsbeat.com, ign.com, nerdist.com, newsarama.com, and theouthousers.com.

This project accesses a Postgres database to receive the output of scraped information.
